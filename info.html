I'm Pregnant. Now What?

If you are pregnant and reading this, we want you to know you’re not alone! We have already prayed for you and have intentionally established Hope Mansion with you in mind. We know right now you are uncertain of what your future looks like. Hope Mansion, where “Life Grows Inside,” is a home away from the confusion, chaos, and pressures of your family and friends, where you can focus on the most important decision of your life - being a parent. While living at Hope Mansion, you will receive both health care, through our medical program, and professional counseling. This will make a significant difference for your own emotional and physical well-being, as well as for your unborn child.

As a new residential group home, Hope Mansion has made careful and deliberate steps towards building a plan that focuses on meeting every one of your needs during this time of your life. 

One of the benefits of living at Hope Mansion is you will be with other women experiencing the same issues as you. Living at Hope Mansion will be a major decision for you. You must be willing and ready to make positive changes in your life, not only for your sake, but for the sake of your child.

Our Program

Hope Mansion exists to break the chain of generational poverty and hopelessness by providing women in crisis pregnancy a loving, structured, residential environment, as well as necessary time, resources, and support for them and their babies to become productive, well-balanced citizens.

The Residential Program at Hope Mansion provides:

  • Housing, meals and resources throughout the duration of pregnancy, and up to 6 weeks after the baby is born 
  • Resident Program 
  • Case management 
  • Daily Classes 
  • Group and Individual Counseling 
  • Mentoring

The criteria for a prospective resident is:

  • Pregnant, age 18 and older (housing is not available for children at this time)

Hope Mansion is more than transitional housing, it is a holistic program where residents can grow emotionally, intellectually, spiritually and through goal setting and planning. Potential residents must be willing to be a part of a program where they can grow and learn.

To get more information, please contact us.

Volunteers

Our mission at Hope Mansion cannot be completed without faithful volunteers. We have many different volunteer opportunities and ways for you and/or your group to get involved!

At Hope Mansion, there is a structured setting were staff and volunteers can facilitate a loving, safe environment for our residents to prepare to have their baby and work on their the long and short term goals. This is accomplished through our Resident Program which includes weekly case management and goal setting, daily classes, mentoring, counseling, basics of how to run a healthy home, how to be a healthy parent (or preparation for adoption) and preparing for the next step in their journey. There are many different ways for you to get involved!

 Volunteer Opportunities:

  • Life Events (Birthdays, Holidays)
  • Community Life (Friday Fun, Field Trips)        
  • Administrative        
  • Prayer Team         
  • Transportation    
  • Teaching Classes       
  • Fundraisers        
  • PR           
  • Mentoring          
  • Baby Care             
  • Facilities
  • Grant Writing       
  • Graphic Design         
  • Cooking Class

Daily, weekly, monthly and annual opportunities are available for individuals and groups. All individuals wishing to serve must be approved Hope Mansion volunteers (application is on our website). For groups that want to serve one time (and will not be interacting with the residents), each volunteer will be asked to sign a liability release.

To volunteer, please complete the below application and return via email at info@hopemansion.org or by mail or in person: Hope Mansion; 1595 Mt. Lebanon Rd.; Cedar Hill, TX 75104

Volunteer Orientation and Training

Orientation – We host new volunteer orientation meetings on the 3rd Tuesday of each month. The meetings are held at Hope Mansion at 6 pm for those that are new or are interested in learning more about volunteering.

Training - Ministering to those that are in a time of crisis in their lives is very rewarding, but we also know it is important to be properly equipped to serve in that way. As part of our volunteer program, we provide quarterly training to better equip you as you serve at Hope Mansion.

If you are currently a volunteer or are interested in learning more, please join us. We will be hearing from Karin Matula as she teaches about the option of adoption and how to minister to that specific need. Please join us and bring a friend!

We host quarterly volunteer training for all volunteers.  If you are currently a volunteer or are interested in learning more, please contact us for more information.
