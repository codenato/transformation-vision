THE MISSION. 

Hope Mansion exists to break the chain of hopelessness by providing women in crisis pregnancy a loving, structured residential environment, as well the necessary time, resources, and support for them and their babies, in order to become productive, well-balanced citizens.

THE NEED.

Currently, the physical and psychological consequences of abortion, abandonment, abuse, loneliness, and poverty are growing in unprecedented measure. Without resources, education, life skills, knowledge of child rearing, or a spiritual foundation, women will likely return to old friends, former ways to life, and eventually more unplanned pregnancies.

THE OUTCOME.

Hope Mansion has been helping single, pregnant women in need of housing and hope since 2012.  Of those women:
- 100% were homeless
- 65% moved forward in their education
- 91% chose single parenting
- 9% chose adoption
