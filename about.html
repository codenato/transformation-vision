Purpose

To unite spiritual, political, business, educational and social leaders to serve and steward the blessings of God in order to achieve our maximum potential.

Mission

To create a collaborative partnership between businesses, churches and other organizations in the community to provide spiritual covering and demonstrate tangible care for those in need.

Values

Servant Leadership
Be an Example of What a Servant Should Be Mark 10:43-45, Phillipians 2:5-7
Excellence
Discipline and Passion to Persevere and Do What Is Right 1 Corinthians 9:24-27
Influence & Unity
People Working Together Can Accomplish More Matthew 18:19, Ecclesiastes 4:12
Authority & Submission
Treating Others With Respect Will Yield Respect and Trust Proverbs 10:17, Ephesians 5:21
Work
Work Hard, Be Your Best, New Opportunities Come Matthew 5:16
Love
Showing Love to Others Pleases God and Feels Good 1 Corinthians 13
Wisdom

Reward

Legacy
Take Pride in What is Left Behind for Others to Follow Galatians 6:6-9
